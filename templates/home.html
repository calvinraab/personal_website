About Me

Hello! I'm Calvin Raab, a data scientist at Booz Allen Hamilton, with a focus on AI explainability. My primary area of interest is enhancing human interpretability of deep learning models. For AI to realize its full societal potential, trust is essential—and that trust can only be earned through model transparency and understanding, which is where my work is focused.

My academic background in economics has equipped me with a valuable framework for decision-making and problem-solving. I have a strong interest in economics and finance, where I've conducted extensive modeling. I’m passionate about creating tools that enhance human experiences and make a meaningful impact.
